A short history of Fur Fur Fur.

Fur Fur Fur started out of the garage of it's founders in 2019. They helped clean and groom friends and families furbabies. In 2020, many more people were spending time at home with thier pets and realized how messy they can be, which lead a spike in grooming services. In 2021 Fur Fur Fur opend a store front in NW Portland where they service up to 100 furbabies a month!

Services Offered

Just a Wash

Wash, Cut and Sanitary Groom
